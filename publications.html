D. Marris, S. Sarvaharman and L. Giuggioli, Exact spatiotemporal dynamics of lattice random walks in hexagonal and honeycomb domains Phys. Rev. E (2023).

In this work we develop a general theory to desbribe exactly diffusive processes in hexagonal geometries. We consider two lattices, the hexagonal and the honeycomb. This may find application in a variety of fields, from exciton transport on the surface of carbon nanotubes, to animal foraging theory.
