Daniel Marris

Welcome to my website. I am a statistical physicist in the final year of my PhD in the lab of Prof. Luca Giuggioli at the University of Bristol. Before that I completed an MEng in Engineering Mathematics at the University of Bristol.

In general, I am interested in non-equilibrium statistical physics, with a key interest in developing new theory for discrete search processes. My output is generally mathematical, however to supplement this I write many stochastic simulations. With this, I am interested in the fast implementation of stochastic simulations, with my programming language of choice being Julia.
